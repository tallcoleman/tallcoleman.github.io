 Where can I find the agenda for a meeting?

Agendas usually go up within a week before a meeting.

  1. Go to http://www.governingcouncil.utoronto.ca/Governing_Council/bac.htm
  2. Click on the board/committee/council you’re looking for
  3. Click on Meeting Agendas and Reports under “Resources”
  4. Click on the year, then the date, and you’ll find a PDF of the agenda

What are the addresses of the locations?

SH COUNCIL CHAMBER

Simcoe Hall, 27 King’s College Cir, University of Toronto - St. George Campus, Toronto, ON M5S

Simcoe Hall is attached to/behind Con Hall.  Go up the big stairs in the lobby to the second floor, then turn right.

UTM COUNCIL CHAMBER

Room 3130, Davis Building, University of Toronto Mississauga 3359 Mississauga Rd, Mississauga, ON L5L 1C6

Go to the Davis Caf (where the big Timmies is), go upstairs, then use the signs/walk around the big glass open bit until you find room 3130.

UTSC COUNCIL CHAMBER

Room 160, Arts & Admin Building - AA, University of Toronto Scarborough 1265 Military Trail, Scarborough, ON, Canada, M1C 1A4

Go to the southern tip of the AA building, the part closest to the bookstore/Science Wing (otherwise known as “the building with the endless hallway of desks”).
